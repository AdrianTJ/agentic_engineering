# Stakeholder narrative

Translate findings into a decision-oriented story.

## Workflow

1. **Lead with the decision.** What does the audience need to decide or do? That is
   the headline, not the methodology.
2. **Structure as situation → complication → resolution.** What's true, what changed
   or is at risk, what you recommend.
3. **Three points, not thirty.** Choose the few findings that actually move the
   decision; everything else is appendix.
4. **Translate jargon.** Replace model/statistical terms with plain consequences
   ("we'd expect ~15% fewer churned accounts"), keeping the rigor in the appendix.
5. **Be honest about uncertainty.** State confidence and the main caveat plainly;
   it builds trust and pre-empts the obvious pushback.

## Output

A one-page narrative: headline recommendation, three supporting points each tied to a
figure, and a short uncertainty/caveats note. This is the input to `build-deck`.
